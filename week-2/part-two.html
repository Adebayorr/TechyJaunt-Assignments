How to Make Fruit Salad

Fruit salad is one of the easiest and healthiest snacks you can make at home. It’s perfect for a sunny afternoon, a quick breakfast, or a light dessert after dinner. The best part about making a fruit salad is that there are no strict rules—you can use whatever fruits are in season or whatever you happen to have in your fridge. It’s colorful, refreshing, and packed with vitamins!

Materials Needed

  • 2 large bananas
  • 1 medium-sized watermelon
  • A handul of red grapes
  • 2 sweet oranges
  • 1 ripe mango
  • A squeeze of fresh lime juice (optional)
  • A tablespoon of honey for extra sweetness

Instructions

  1. Wash the fruit: Start by thoroughly washing the grapes, oranges, and mango under cool running water to make sure everything is clean.
  2. Peel and chop: Peel the bananas, mango, and oranges. Cut them into small, bite-sized chunks so they are easy to eat with a spoon.
  3. Prepare the watermelon: Slice the watermelon and remove the seeds, then chop the flesh into cubes similar in size to the other fruits.
  4. Mix it up: Place all the chopped fruit and the grapes into a large glass bowl.
  5. Add the dressing: Drizzle the honey and the lime juice over the fruit to keep them fresh and add a bit of a zing
  6. Toss and serve: Use a large spoon to gently toss the fruits together so the flavors mix. You can serve it immediately or let it chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
